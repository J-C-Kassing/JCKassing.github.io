**Venue**:
I am currently organizing Unravel's final symposium which is the final event of the DFG-Research Training Group UnRAVeL at RWTH Aachen University. 
As the closing event of the DFG Research Training Group UnRAVeL, we will be hosting a three-day symposium at RWTH Aachen University.
The symposium will take place from May 27th to May 29th 2026 with an additional welcome reception in the evening of the 26th of May. It will be open to both internal and external participants.

Registration is open until 1st of May. In particular, we cordially invite researchers to register who work on areas related to UnRAVeL: Algorithms and Complexity, Verification and Logic as well as Optimisation under Uncertainty, and applications that deal with uncertainty in data (a non-exhaustive list includes network science, robotics, controlling, or railway infrastructure)!
